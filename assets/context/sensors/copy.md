## How It Works

| Element | Text |
|---------|------|
| Section title | From ward bed to nurse station in 5 seconds |

| Step | Icon | Title | Description |
|------|------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | `Headphones` | Position | Slides onto the forehead. No adhesive, no gel, no wires. Elastic silicone maintains consistent sensor contact. |
| 2 | `Zap` | Detect | Four sensors sample simultaneously — pulse at 200 Hz, temperature at 1 Hz, acoustics at 16 kHz. |
| 3 | `Cpu` | Process | On-board signal fusion rejects motion artifacts and weights channels by SNR. Under 50ms latency. |
| 4 | `Bell` | Alert | Composite vital signs transmit every 5 seconds via BLE 5.0 to bedside dashboard, nurse station, and EHR. |
