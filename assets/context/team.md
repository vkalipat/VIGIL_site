# Team

## Members

| Name | Role | Bio |
|------|------|-----|
| Shaunak Buche | Co-Founder & Product Strategy Lead | Shaunak is interested in the intersection of healthcare, public policy, and technology, particularly how innovative products can improve patient safety and equity. In building VIGIL, he shaped the overall product vision and impact focus, translating clinical needs into clear product requirements. He ensured the solution remained accessible, scalable, and aligned with real hospital workflows. |
| Vishwa Rajan | Co-Founder & Hardware Design Lead | Vishwa is interested in applied engineering and human-centered hardware design. In the VIGIL project, he led physical design and prototyping of the headband, prioritizing comfort, wearability, and durability for patients. His work helped make the device unobtrusive while suitable for continuous monitoring. |
| Vedant Kalipatnapu | Co-Founder & Biomedical Systems Lead | Vedant is passionate about biomedical engineering and data-driven health solutions. For VIGIL, he contributed to the development of the physiological sensing system, focusing on reliable signal capture and interpretation. He researched sensor feasibility and helped refine how the device would function in real clinical environments. |
| Vedant Mehta | Co-Founder & Business Development Lead | Vedant is drawn to entrepreneurship and health technology, with a focus on scalable solutions. For VIGIL, he led market research and competitive analysis, helping position the product within existing hospital systems. He also supported early business-model development to strengthen VIGIL's path from prototype to adoption. |
